I am a computational evolutionary biologist in the Department of Biology at Duke University. Previously, I was the Bioinformatics Project Manager and Training Coordinator at the (sadly-defunct) National Evolutionary Synthesis Center, NESCent.

{% include image.html url="/images/opentree_logo.png" href="https://tree.opentreeoflife.org" width=200 align="left" %}

Most of my time is spend on the Open Tree of Life project, which builds a comprehensive tree of all life by synthesizing phylogeny and taxonomy. Browse the tree of life on the opentree website; submit and curate published trees for inclusion; or find opentree code on github

When not building the tree of life, I help teach software and data skills through Software and Data Carpentry. When not sitting a desk, I am often found riding ponies.
